Onboarding



Welcome to VETS!! We're so glad to have you!
We're a relaxed, social-first, low-obligation guild, which means everything we do - wars, events, and all that is completely optional. No pressure, ever!

Once you're settled in, you'll have access to our library of teleport scrolls. Feel free to use them anytime, but remember to just pop them back when you're done! And don't forget to swing by our awesome guild island!! (it's definitely worth a visit).

We run a fun new event every week, and they're a great way to earn guild rewards. You can find all the details on our Discord, and of course, they're totally optional, so join in whenever you feel like it!

Most importantly, just be kind, have fun, and try to hop on at least once every two weeks so you don't get marked inactive. But don't worry, if you ever do get kicked for inactivity, you're always welcome back with open arms.

Once again, welcome to VETS! We're excited to have you with us!!!!!
